About the Game

The city has been consumed by a malevolent fog, its streets and buildings hidden beneath an impenetrable shroud of darkness. Once vibrant and alive, the metropolis now exists in a state of eerie silence, its true form concealed from the world.

You are the last hope. Ancient fragments of light, scattered throughout the city, hold the power to pierce through the evil fog and reveal what lies beneath. These luminous shards are the key to unmasking the city and restoring it to its former glory.

Your Mission

Navigate the fog-covered city and collect all the fragments of light in the shortest time possible. As you explore, the fog gradually lifts, revealing more of the city's secrets.

How to Play

  • Move: WASD or Arrow keys
  • Look around: Mouse
  • Sprint: Hold Shift (drains stamina)
  • Slide: Ctrl while sprinting (speed boost)
  • Jump: Space
  • Mute audio: M
  • Pause: Esc

Tips

  • Follow the compass arrow - it points to the nearest fragment
  • The beeping sound gets faster as you approach a fragment
  • Explore thoroughly to maximize your exploration percentage
  • Manage your stamina - sprinting drains it quickly
